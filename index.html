Our History

Biarritz & Surfing is a recent company. Our vision of surfing is different. Indeed the most important in our surf lessons is the level you are starting from and your age.We distinguish ourselves from our competitors by the presence of courses according to the age of the participants to make this sport more fun. And create new links around a common passion.

Our project

We offer two offers, the formula with integrated license and the formula without for premises. Our lessons last an hour and a half and it is possible to do up to four per day. A course with a license costs thirty euros and a course without a license costs twenty euros.
